Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Short version: Multi Converter does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device.

1. What We Do Not Collect

Multi Converter does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any personal or financial data

There is no account registration, no login, and no user tracking of any kind.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only (using browser localStorage / app storage):

  • Your selected currencies
  • Your preferred theme (dark / light)
  • Your preferred decimal mode

This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

3. Exchange Rate Data

To show you live exchange rates, the app makes a network request to a third-party exchange rate API:

  • currency-api.pages.dev — provides daily updated exchange rates

This request contains no personal information. Only your device's IP address is involved, as with any normal internet request.

4. Flag Images

Country flag images are loaded from flagcdn.com, a public flag image service. These requests contain no personal information.

5. Advertising and Analytics

Multi Converter contains no advertisements and uses no analytics services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any third-party tracking tools.

6. Children's Privacy

Multi Converter does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy in a meaningful way, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since we collect no data, significant changes are unlikely.
